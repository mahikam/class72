places must visit

aruba

I had visited Aruba in December 21 to December 28 in 2019.

This picture was taken on a private island owned by the hotel my family and I were staying at. To access this island we had to take a small boat. There were flamingos and a clear, blue beach. On the second day at the island we rented a cabana where we were allowed to stay the whole day, which we did until the sun started to set. There was a water slide, a hammock, and a lounge area with couches in the cabana.



maui, hawaii

I visited the island, Maui, December 2018 to January 2019.

I had taken this picture once we had gotten off a boat on a dock. In the ocean were a pile of rocks and the water was such a deep blue. The sky was also extremely clear that day.



kauai, hawaii

While in Hawaii I also visited Kauai from December 2018 to January 2019.

When we visited Hawaii we went to two islands, Kauai and Maui. Kauai's water was practically see through when the waves were calm and the island's turtle population was huge among the coasts. But when the waves aren't calm and it was extremely windy it was still beautiful.

review on movies

The Purge
One of my favorite horror series!

This movie keeps you on your toes and anxious throughout the film. I watched this film with my friends and the expierience was amazing. The concept is very creative too! For 12 hours all crime, including murder, is legal and one family unfortunetly made a mistake that could cause them their lives.

Halloween
Never anything but thrilling!

I watched this movie with my mom and it never had a dull moment. Micheal Myers escapes jail 40 years after attempting and failing to murder Laurie Strode. After becoming free he goes after the reason he's in jail, Laurie Strode.

Scream
A horror classic!

This was the first movie I ever watched that was a horror movie. I watched this with my mom and it was a classic horror movie. A masked maniac with a knife stalks and murders highschool students and this movie shows that in the horror genre you never know who to trust.
